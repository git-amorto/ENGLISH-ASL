About

FAQs

What exactly is this ASL project with Rajiv Sir?

It's a unique classroom initiative where we watch movies, engage in discussions, and work on our speaking skills in a relaxed and enjoyable setting.

What can I expect during these ASL sessions?

Get ready for movie screenings followed by lively discussions. We'll explore themes, characters, and practice speaking skills along the way.

Is this like an extempore or a debate?

Not exactly! It's more about casual conversations inspired by the movies we watch. Think of it as a friendly exchange of ideas and opinions.

Will there be assignments or tests?

Nope! No formal assessments here. It's all about learning through conversation and enjoying the movie experience together.

How does fire suppression impact ecosystems?

Historically, fire suppression disrupted natural fire cycles, leading to fuel buildup and larger, more intense wildfires. It also altered ecosystems, affecting plant and animal communities that depend on fire for their survival. A balanced fire management approach has emerged to mitigate these impacts.

Can I suggest movies to watch during the sessions?

Absolutely! We love recommendations. Feel free to share your favorite movies, and we'll add them to our watchlist.

How long do these ASL sessions last?

Typically, sessions run for about an hour or so. Enough time to watch a movie and have a good discussion afterward..
